# About Me

Hi, I'm **Melnard De Jesus** — a passionate **Full-Stack Web Developer** and **Backend Engineer** who specializes in creating high-performance **Server-Side Rendering (SSR)** and **Incremental Static Regeneration (ISR)** solutions for marketing websites, web applications, and enterprise backend systems.

I have extensive experience building scalable backend architectures using **Nest.js**, **Node.js**, **Express.js**, and modern JavaScript/TypeScript frameworks like **Astro**, **React**, and **Next.js**. I specialize in developing **AI-powered applications**, **SaaS platforms**, **property management systems**, and **e-commerce solutions** with a strong focus on performance, scalability, and seamless third-party integrations.

## Things I've Built

- **Property Management System** — a full platform for managing properties and bookings, with AI-generated offers powered by the **OpenAI API** and **Stripe** handling payments and subscriptions.
- **NetSuite AI Assistant** — a conversational assistant for NetSuite backed by a **custom MCP server**, exposing NetSuite data and operations as tools the model can call directly.
